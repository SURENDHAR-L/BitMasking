🌟 Aptitude Shortcut Mastery Roadmap (4 Weeks Plan)
🗓️ Week 1 – Core Arithmetic Speed & Accuracy

🧮 Goal: Build mental agility for basic number operations.

Topics:

Addition Shortcuts

Left-to-right addition

Rounding and compensating method

Column-wise addition (for large numbers)

Subtraction Shortcuts

Balancing technique

Borrow-free subtraction

Rounding method

Multiplication Shortcuts

Vedic “Nikhilam” method (numbers near 10, 100, 1000)

Vertically & crosswise method

Multiplying by 5, 25, 50, 75, 125

Squares of numbers ending in 5

Shortcuts for numbers with 11, 12, 13, etc.

Division Shortcuts

Short division method

Division by 5, 25, 125

Approximation division for large numbers

Remainder-based tricks

🧠 Focus: Practice until you can handle any 2-digit or 3-digit calculation mentally.

🗓️ Week 2 – Power Numbers & Roots

⚙️ Goal: Handle squares, cubes, and roots efficiently.

Topics:

Squares

Memorize 1²–30²

Shortcut for (a + b)²

Squaring numbers close to base (like 50², 48², etc.)

Square Roots

Finding square root of perfect squares quickly

Estimation for imperfect squares (√50 ≈ 7.07)

Using difference of squares for approximation

Cubes

Memorize 1³–20³

Using (a + b)³ formula for quick cube

Shortcut for cubes near base (like 100³ type)

Cube Roots

Identifying cube root of perfect cubes (based on last digit pattern)

Logical cube root estimation for near-perfect cubes

🧠 Focus: Build fluency with mental recognition of square and cube patterns.

🗓️ Week 3 – Percentages, Ratios, and Fractions

📊 Goal: Strengthen the link between arithmetic and aptitude reasoning.

Topics:

Percentage Tricks

Convert fractions ↔ percentages (like 1/6 = 16.66%)

Fast percentage increase/decrease

Successive percentage formula

Fractions Simplification

Memorize common fractions up to 1/20

Finding fraction equivalents mentally

Ratio & Proportion

Shortcut ratio comparison using cross multiplication

Mean & continued proportion shortcuts

Averages

Weighted average shortcut

Combining averages

🧠 Focus: Achieve comfort converting between fraction, ratio, and percentage forms instantly.

🗓️ Week 4 – Applied Speed Math & Number Sense

🚀 Goal: Apply shortcuts in aptitude-style problems.

Topics:

Time & Work / Time & Distance Calculations

Shortcut LCM-based methods

Ratio-based solving for speed/work

Efficiency tricks

Simplification & Approximation

Use rounding and Vedic math

Quick fraction-to-decimal approximations

Number System Shortcuts

Divisibility rules (for 2–11)

Remainder and cyclicity tricks

Unit digit and last two-digit shortcuts

Miscellaneous Tricks

Difference of squares & cubes

Square of sum / difference formulas

Simple interest vs compound interest approximations

🧠 Focus: Integrate your speed tricks into real aptitude-style questions.

🎯 Final Week (Optional Revision Week)

If you have 5 weeks, use the last one for:
